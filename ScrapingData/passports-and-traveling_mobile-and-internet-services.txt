Contact Etisalat or du before you leave the UAE for mobile and internet packages while travelling abroad.

Getting mobile and internet services
Etisalat  and  du  offer roaming mobile phone and internet services abroad. Contact them for their latest offering. You can call Etisalat on 101 or 144 and du on 155.
