Social assistance
Orphans and children from unknown parents receive monthly financial assistance from the UAE Government.
According to   the Federal Law No (2) of 2001 on social security a monthly assistance  is provided to the following categories of UAE nationals residing within the UAE:
the Federal Law No (2) of 2001 on social security
