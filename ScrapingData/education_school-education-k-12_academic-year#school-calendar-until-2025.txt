Academic year
The academic year in the UAE starts in September and ends in June. There are two shorts breaks and a long summer vacation.

Three terms in an academic year
The academic year has been divided into trimesters since the academic year 2010-2011. All public and private schools in the UAE must offer the minimum number of instruction days set out by Ministry of Education.
