Healthcare budget
Healthcare is one of the most important service sectors in the UAE and holds special position in the government’s agenda. This page provides information about the budget allocated for the health care sector in the UAE.
Healthcare is one of the most important service sectors in the UAE and holds special position in the government’s agenda. This page provides information about the budget allocated for the health care sector in the UAE.
