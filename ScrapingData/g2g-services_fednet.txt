Federal Network (FedNet)
Federal Network (FedNet) was launched to transform the UAE into a globally recognised mobile government that provides the best services to the people at any location, round the clock. In addition, FedNet is aligned with Vision 2021 that aims to make the UAE one of the best countries in the world by the year 2021. Refer to the FedNet service catalogue (PDF, 1 MB). The FedNet project was implemented in consequence of the National Plan to Support mGovernment Initiative to provide a common and smart infrastructure.
